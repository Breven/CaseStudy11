    Top 5 golfing tips

  • Choose the correct club

    • Using the best club for the given situation will help you get the most out of your swing.

    A driver is best used for hitting the ball off the tee or long-range shots. An iron is used to get close to the green or out of a sand wedge.

    And a putter is used for when you are on the green and a few yards away from the hole.

  • Relax

    • This can be the best tip to remember, as getting frustrated or overthinking can impair your swings and make you hate the game.

    Try to avoid being intimidated by the course, and enjoy playing.

  • Take your stance

    • A proper stance can help you maintain your balance and improve your swings. Position your feet shoulder-width apart, with your weight

    on the balls of your feet. Bend your knees slightly while keeping your spine straight. Avoid holding the club too tightly, and keep your

    body relaxed

  • Swinging basics

    • The power in your swing comes from your body, not your arms. After taking the correct stance, bring the club behind you while twisting

    your body. Then, swing the club downwards parallel to the ground while releasing your twist. You want to be facing perpendicular to the

    ball when you make contact.

  • Putting

    • After getting set up for your shot, keep your eyes on the hole. This allows you to focus more on your target than hitting the ball.
